ABOUT

"Tenet" is a mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows a protagonist known only as "The Protagonist" as he embarks on a mission to prevent World War III through the manipulation of time. As he delves deeper into the world of espionage and inversion, he must navigate a complex web of secrets, technology, and danger. With stunning visuals, intricate plotting, and a unique concept of time inversion, "Tenet" keeps audiences on the edge of their seats until the very end.
